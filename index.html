About Rosalie Bay Rum

Rosalie Bay Rum is a premium Dominican cane juice rum that embodies the essence of Dominica's land, people, and conservation efforts. Our handcrafted organic rum is produced using traditional methods with a focus on sustainability and exceptional quality.

Every bottle you purchase directly supports our conservation work to protect sea turtles, whales, and coral reefs in Dominica. We're not just making rum - we're creating a sustainable financial foundation for long-term marine conservation.

"Every bottle of our handcrafted organic rum is a reflection of the land, the people, and the traditions of Dominica. From the moment the cane is hand cut to the final proofing with pristine mountain spring water, every step is intentional—honouring heritage, sustainability, and the pursuit of exceptional flavour."

Our rum is made with four heirloom cane varieties, each with a unique flavor profile, all grown without any synthetic fertilizers or pesticides, preserving the integrity of the land and the purity of our rum.
