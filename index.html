Farmer Payment Analysis

This visualization tracks payments made to our network of local sugarcane farmers throughout Dominica. Our data shows the distribution across different villages and individual farmers, highlighting our commitment to fair compensation and supporting local agriculture.

Interactive Chart Loading...

Over the 2023-2024 period, we've made payments to over 80 farmers across more than 15 villages in Dominica. This data provides insights into our supply chain and demonstrates our commitment to supporting local farmers.

Key Insights

  • La Plaine leads with approximately $76,000 in payments, followed by Belles ($40,000) and Wesley ($33,000)
  • The top 3 villages account for about 60% of all payments
  • Within villages, there's typically a top farmer who receives significantly more than others
  • La Plaine has both the highest total payments and the highest individual farmer payments
  • Smaller villages often have more even payment distribution among farmers
