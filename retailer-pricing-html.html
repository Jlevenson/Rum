PREMIUM POSITIONING INFORMATION

Quality Differentiators

  • Organic Heirloom Sugarcane: Four rare varieties grown in small family farms
  • Handcrafted Process: No industrial machinery, ensuring authentic traditional methods
  • Premium Distillation: Double pot-distilled with careful hearts selection
  • Pure Spring Water: Proofed using pristine mountain spring water from just 1,300 feet from the distillery
  • Conservation Mission: Every bottle sold supports marine conservation initiatives

Retailer Benefits

  • Unique Product Story: Stand out with a rum that has authentic conservation impact

  • Craft Category Growth: Capitalize on the growing craft and premium rum market

Display Recommendations

  • Showcase as a premium shelf offering alongside other high-end rums
