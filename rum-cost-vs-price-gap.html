Just Production + Raw Materials ($326.31) Already Exceed Our Prices

Even if we only counted the costs of production and raw materials, these two expenses alone exceed both our retail and wholesale prices:

  • Production + Raw Materials = $326.31
  • This is 3.3 times our retail price ($100.00)
  • This is 5.0 times our wholesale price ($65.00)

Why Do We Do This?

Even though each bottle costs more to make than we sell it for, we're building something special:

  • We're developing a premium brand that will be worth more in the future
  • We're supporting local farmers and workers in Dominica
  • We're funding conservation of sea turtles, whales, and coral reefs
  • As we make more bottles, our cost per bottle will decrease significantly
  • We're investing in sustainable, high-quality production methods

How We'll Become Profitable

To break even, we need to either:

Increase production to
~4,300 bottles per year

(at retail price of $100)

OR

Charge $434.49 per bottle

(keeping the same production)

We'll likely do a combination of both - make more bottles while gradually increasing prices as our reputation grows.
